About Me

My name is Christopher Dickerson, but I go by Preston. I am a Computer Science major at Centre College. I enjoy being challenged in my everyday life, whether it comes to academics or athletics. I thrive in high-pressure situations and am able to overcome my obstacles by thinking outside the box. 

I have had some amazing opportunities to show off my coding skill, one of those being at my most recent internship at Wilderness Trace Solar. I showed my ability to problem solve and create plans to better improve customer experience on websites. I also had the opportunity to improve the internal database of Wilderness Trace Solar using automating scripts and improved structuring.

 I am also an athlete at Centre College running both Cross Country and Track where I have earned multiple awards. I am very proud to be a part of a very competitive team and I constantly have to work to keep up. I've always enjoyed challenges and this is a great example of that.
